Kerala

Kochi-Munnar-Alleppey

Explore Kerala through Kochi, Munnar, and Alappuzha. Start in Kochi with its colonial charm, including Fort Kochi’s fishing nets, Mattancherry Palace, and the Jewish Synagogue. Next, visit Munnar, a lush hill station famous for its tea plantations and views. Wander tea estates, spot the Nilgiri Tahr at Eravikulam National Park, and enjoy Anamudi Peak. End in Alappuzha, the "Venice of the East," with serene backwaters, houseboats, and Aleppey Beach.

Kochi-Alleppey-Vagamon

Discover South India’s charm with our Kochi-Alleppey-Vagamon tour! Start in Kochi, where streets meet stunning waterways, showcasing rich culture and heritage. Then, relax on a houseboat through Alleppey’s serene backwaters and picturesque landscapes. Conclude in Vagamon, a hidden gem in the Western Ghats with lush meadows and rolling hills. This tour blends cultural exploration with natural beauty, ensuring a memorable and exciting experience. Book now for an unforgettable adventure!

Kochi-Munnar-Thekkady-Alleppey

Discover South India's beauty with our Kochi-Munnar-Thekkady-Alleppey tour. Start in Kochi, a vibrant city with historical sites, lively markets, and scenic waterfronts. Then, head to Munnar, a serene hill station known for its tea plantations and misty landscapes. Next, visit Thekkady and explore the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary with its dense forests and wildlife, enjoying safaris and boat rides. Finally, relax in Alleppey on a traditional houseboat, gliding through tranquil backwaters surrounded by lush greenery.
